How to treat water

Note: do not drink water with unusual smells or colors.

  1. Strain water with a cloth, paper towel, or coffee filter
  2. Boil your water
  3. Use portible water filters
  4. Use disinfectants (refer to resources below for the proper types of disinfectants and ratios)
